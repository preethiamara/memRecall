Information and tools to help those with Alzheimers

Information

Alzheimers is a disease that affects people's ability to retain short-term memory, and eventually, their ability to perform basic tasks.

The causes of Alzheimers are not fully understood, but it is likely due to a combination of age related changes in the brain, such as blood vessel damage, cell death, changes in genes, and various lifestyle factors, such as blood ressure and obesity.

The most frequent symptoms are: difficulty in remembering, forgettng simple words, spatial issues, and impaired reasoning. Some other sympotoms are repeating questions, wandering, misplacing things, increased anxiety, and personality changes.

Note: Having one or more of these symptoms does not mean one certianly has Alzheimer's, but it does make it more likely. Please check with a doctor if you're unsure.

Tools to assist in living with Alzheimers

Though there is no cure for Alzheimer's, there is some treatment avaliable. Most medicines work in the early or middle stages, and research is being done to other treatments to delay or prevent the disease. Ask your doctor for more information.

Relax and exercise your mind

In the games page there are three games each designed to improve brain functions such as attention, concentration, memory, and focus. In the contacts page you are able to add an unlimited number of contacts along with basic information to help you identify your loved ones. This page also includes a search bar that allows you to search through your contacts. In the resources page you will find information about Alzheimer's, tools to assist those with the disease, and a refreshable fun and healthy daily activity.
